Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

Data Collection and Use

Schedule Ninja prioritizes your privacy. All AI processing is performed directly on your device using Chrome's on-device AI.

Information We Collect

  • Selected webpage text (for the purpose of extracting schedule information)
  • User settings (language, timezone, theme, etc.)
  • Extension usage statistics (anonymized data)

Data Security

All data is stored locally in your browser and is not sent to external servers. AI processing works completely offline using Chrome's built-in AI features.

Google Calendar Permissions

Schedule Ninja requests minimal permissions to add events to your Google Calendar. It only accesses the calendar when you explicitly approve and does not read or modify existing events.

Third-Party Sharing

Schedule Ninja does not share, sell, or rent your data to third parties. All data is kept securely on your device.

Compliance with Chrome Web Store Policies

Schedule Ninja's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@scheduleninja.com.
